Controls
=====================

Basic Controls
--------------

Katrina is a bruiser type with 4 buttons.  

---Standing----
x			- Poke
y			- Bite
a			- Front Kick
b			- Tail Whip 
x+a			- Grab/Throw

---Crouching---
x			- Low Swipe
y			- Upper Bite
a			- Low Kick
b			- Tail Sweep


---Jumping---
x			- Swipe
y			- Bite
a			- Front Kick
b			- Drop Kick

---specials---
Charge back, Forward + p	- Bust Rush
Charge down, Up + P 		- Bust Swing
HCB + P	 			- Death Roll Suplex (Grab)
QCF + k(air only)		- Moonsault
y+b				- Rachelle Assist

---supers---
2 x QCF +  p 		- Crocodile Sandwich
c+z

---Defensive---		
Durring Block:
x+y			- Chest bump (Push block)
				1/5 Bar needed

---Movement---

F,F			- Sprint
D,U			- Super Jump 
B,B 			- Backdash
