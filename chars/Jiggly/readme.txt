Jiggly Bunny is a rush down type character who has uses her abundant body to her advantage.  She has below average health but
a good amount of speed and combo potential.

Controls
=====================

Basic Controls
--------------

Jiggly will be a 4 button character and so far only has her normals.

---Standing----
x			- Slap (x2)
y			- Haymaker
a			- Thick kick (x2)
b			- Bunny Kick 
x+a			-Grab/Throw

---Crouching---
x			- Low punch
y			- Uppercut
a			- Low Kick
b			- Sweep


---Jumping---
x			- Double palm
y			- light kick
x			- straight punch
y 			- Hip check (can be done twice)
a (tap)			- Mario's F. Air (Meteors)
a (hold)		- Mario's F. Air (Always Ground bounces)
b			- Butt Drop
up 			- Air Jump (x2)
D + b 			- Dive butt

---specials---
QCB + k			- Flipping Kick (bypasses projectiles)
QCF + k 		- Butt Bump (high damage body projectile)
DP + k 			- Anti Air Ass 

---supers---
2 x QCF +  k 		- Lovely Thump (air only)
c+z

---Defensive---		
Durring Block:
x+y			- Dodge Roll (can go forward or backward)
				1/2 Bar needed

---Movement---

F,F			- Sprint
D,U			- Super Jump 
U (in air)		- Tripple Jump
B,B 			- Backflip (invunerable)
U (while running)	- Long Jump
Combos
-----------------------

She's very combo friendly.  Experiment with her and you'll find stuff.
